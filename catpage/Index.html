Why a Cat?

Although many people have dogs, cats are just as cool and friendly!

  • There are nearly 19 million stray cats out on the streets, in need of a home.
  • Cats can keep pests away from your home.
  • Be the one to make a difference in a cats life.

Meats, not Sweets

Did you know cats can't taste sweetness? Here are some treats that they cant taste:

Cake

A carefully crafted motivational device that may or may not exist.

Candy

A sweet food that features sugar as the primary ingredient

Donut

A small fried cake of sweetened dough, usually in the shape of a ring.

Popsicle

A piece of flavored ice or icecream on a stick
